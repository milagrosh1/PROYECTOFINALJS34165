Welcome to my photography studio!

Was planned as a 500 m2 creative powerhouse, a meeting point and cyclical exchange in San Isidro, Buenos Aires. We aspire to constant innovation, which is why we have a variety of spaces designed to encourage creative development: we offer shared office rentals and coworking, as well as five filming sets and photographic studios (an underwater studio, a rooftop, a studio with a sunroof and another of large dimensions for formidable productions), suitable for corporate, business and social events. Our spaces adapt and coexist as an art gallery since we have different studios for photographic books and we are capable of providing a complete photographic service. The name designated for the complex is, without a doubt, a foretaste of the atmosphere that is breathed in the building, a unique square with a creative, original and innovative capacity that seeks to be the change from within and influence the rest through its renewing thought. Within a city overwhelmed by the fast-paced lifestyle, IAN F. PHOTOGRAPHY works as a parenthesis, an interruption of time and space. You can contact us for: Baptisms - Birthdays - Weddings - Corporate Events - Social Events - Advertising Campaigns - Product Photo. Whatever you can think of we can fulfill it.
